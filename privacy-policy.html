Privacy Policy for Etsy Product Poster App

Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Company Information

This application (Etsy Product Poster) is created and operated by Aqueous Art Co.

2. Data Access and Use

This app accesses your Pinterest account solely to automatically post new products from your Etsy RSS feed as Pins to your Pinterest board. The required permissions are:

  • boards:read - To see your boards.
  • pins:write - To create Pins on your behalf.
  • user_accounts:read - To identify your account.

We do not store, share, or sell any of your personal data.
