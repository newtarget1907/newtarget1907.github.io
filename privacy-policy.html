What Data We Access

This app (Etsy Product Poster) is operated by Aqueous Art Co. The app accesses your Pinterest account only to:

  • Read your Pinterest boards (boards:read)
  • Create pins on your boards (pins:write)
  • Read your user account information (user_accounts:read)

How We Use Your Data

The app automatically posts new products from your Etsy RSS feed as pins to your selected Pinterest board. No user data is stored, shared, or sold to any third party.

Data Retention

This app does not store any personal data. All operations are performed in real-time through the Pinterest API.
