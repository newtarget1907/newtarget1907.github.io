Privacy Policy for Etsy Product Poster App

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Company Information

Company Name: Aqueous Art Co

Website: https://www.etsy.com/shop/AqueousArtCo

What Data We Access

This app (Etsy Product Poster) accesses your Pinterest account only to:

  • Read your Pinterest boards (boards:read)
  • Create pins on your boards (pins:write)
  • Read your user account information (user_accounts:read)

How We Use Your Data

The app automatically posts new products from your Etsy RSS feed as pins to your selected Pinterest board. No user data is stored, shared, or sold to any third party.

Data Retention

This app does not store any personal data. All operations are performed in real-time through the Pinterest API.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact: aqueousartco@gmail.com

This privacy policy applies only to the Etsy Product Poster app on Pinterest.
